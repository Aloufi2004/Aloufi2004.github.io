Why Dubai?

Dubai is very popular place for tourists. Many people go there becuase of the amazing food, view, and activities. Dubai is a diverse city. You will find people from all around the world. Also, you will see alot of sport cars there. Dubai is an expensive city to live in, that's why many people who live there are rich.







Dubai is home of the tallest building in the whole world. The building is know as Burj Khalifa.
The building's height is 2,717′, 2,722′ to tip. The cost of building Burj Khalifa is 1.7 billion USD
